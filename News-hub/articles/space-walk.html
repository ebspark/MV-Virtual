SpaceX: Space Walk

SpaceX makes a exciting annoucement! SpaceX reveals that they are doing their space walk. This will take place on 9/12/24 at about 5:58 a.m. ET according to NBC News!

What can we expect to go wrong

Many people are saying it will be one of the largest events for a while in space exploration. However, many people don't know how dangerous space can be. Many things can go wrong with the engines, the cables, their suits, and cabin pressure. We hope the astronauts make it up and come back down safely.

More Event Details And Planning for the future

According to The New York Times, Jared Isaacman, a billionaire entrepreneur, and SpaceX engineer Sarah Gillis are the astronauts taking on this mission. Hopefully, the first space walk ends well. Looking ahead, SpaceX wants to adopt these astronaut suits for possible moon missions and future Mars missions. SpaceX, currently one of the leading space travel companies, says it would take about six months from the time we leave Earth to get to Mars.
